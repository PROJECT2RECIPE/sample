Pepper Chicken

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 tablespoons black peppercorns, coarsely crushed
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 10-12 curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Preparation Steps

  1. Heat Oil & Saut√©: Heat oil in a pan. Add sliced onions, green chilies, and curry leaves. Saut√© until onions turn golden brown.
  2. Add Ginger-Garlic Paste: Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook until raw smell disappears.
  3. Add Spices: Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  4. Add Chicken: Add chicken pieces and stir to coat with the spices. Cook on medium heat until chicken turns white.
  5. Add Pepper & Garam Masala: Sprinkle the crushed black pepper and garam masala. Mix thoroughly and cover to cook for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Cook Until Tender: Cook until the chicken is tender and the oil starts to separate from the masala.
  7. Garnish & Serve: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves. Serve hot with steamed rice, roti, or your favorite bread. Enjoy the spicy, aromatic pepper chicken!

Paneer Butter Masala

Paneer Butter Masala is a rich and creamy North Indian curry made with soft paneer cubes simmered in a luscious tomato-based gravy, enriched with butter, cream, and aromatic spices. Perfect with naan or steamed rice! üßàüçÖüßÄ

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer, cut into cubes
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 large tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves), crushed
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional)
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Preparation Steps

  1. Saut√© Onions: Heat oil and 1 tbsp butter in a pan. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  2. Add Ginger-Garlic Paste: Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes until aromatic.
  3. Add Tomato Puree & Spices: Add tomato puree, red chili powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook on medium heat until oil separates from the masala.
  4. Add Paneer: Gently add paneer cubes and mix well to coat with the masala.
  5. Add Cream & Butter: Stir in fresh cream, remaining butter, kasuri methi, garam masala, and sugar. Cook for another 5 minutes on low heat.
  6. Garnish & Serve: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves. Serve hot with naan, roti, or steamed rice.

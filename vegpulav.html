Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 4-5 green cardamom pods
  • 4 cloves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans, potatoes, chopped)
  • 1 green chili, slit (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups water
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Preparation Steps

  1. Rinse Rice: Rinse basmati rice 2-3 times until water runs clear. Soak for 20 minutes and drain.
  2. Heat Oil/Ghee: In a deep pan, heat oil or ghee. Add bay leaf, cardamom, cloves, and cinnamon. Saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Saut√© Onions: Add sliced onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  4. Add Ginger-Garlic Paste: Stir in ginger-garlic paste and green chili, cook for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Add Vegetables & Spices: Add mixed vegetables, turmeric, garam masala, and salt. Cook for 3-4 minutes.
  6. Add Rice: Add the drained rice and gently mix to coat with the spices and vegetables.
  7. Cook Pulao: Pour in water, bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes until rice is cooked and water absorbed.
  8. Rest & Garnish: Turn off heat, let pulao rest covered for 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork and garnish with fresh coriander.
  9. Serve: Serve hot with raita, pickle, or salad.

Rawa Idly

Rawa Idly is a quick and easy South Indian steamed savory cake made from semolina (rawa), yogurt, and spices. It‚Äôs a delicious breakfast or snack, soft and fluffy, served with chutney and sambar. üçöüåø

Ingredients

  • 1 cup semolina (rawa/suji)
  • 1 cup plain yogurt (curd)
  • 1/2 cup water (adjust as needed)
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • 1 tsp urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 10-12 curry leaves
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda or fruit salt (optional, for fluffiness)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee

Preparation Steps

  1. Roast Semolina: Dry roast semolina in a pan on medium heat until it turns light golden and fragrant. Set aside to cool.
  2. Prepare Tempering: Heat oil in a small pan, add mustard seeds, chana dal, urad dal, and curry leaves. Saut√© until dals turn golden.
  3. Mix Batter: In a bowl, combine yogurt, water, salt, grated ginger, chopped green chilies, chopped coriander, and the roasted semolina. Add the tempering and mix well.
  4. Rest Batter: Let the batter rest for 15-20 minutes to allow semolina to soften.
  5. Add Leavening: Just before steaming, add baking soda or fruit salt to the batter and gently mix.
  6. Steam Idlis: Grease idli molds with oil and pour the batter into them. Steam in an idli steamer or pressure cooker (without whistle) for 10-12 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean.
  7. Serve: Remove the idlis carefully and serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium potatoes (peeled and diced)
  • 2 tbsp oil (vegetable or coconut oil)
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 8-10 curry leaves
  • 1 green chili, chopped (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Preparation Steps

  1. Boil Potatoes: Boil the diced potatoes until just tender but not mushy. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat Oil: In a pan, heat oil and add mustard seeds. Let them splutter.
  3. Add Curry Leaves & Chili: Add curry leaves and green chili, sautÃ© for a few seconds.
  4. Spices: Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and garam masala. Stir well.
  5. Add Potatoes: Add boiled potatoes, salt, and mix gently to coat the potatoes with spices.
  6. Fry: Cook on medium heat, stirring occasionally, until potatoes turn golden and slightly crispy (about 10 minutes).
  7. Serve: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot as a side dish.

Egg Bhurji

Egg Bhurji is a popular Indian-style scrambled eggs dish cooked with onions, tomatoes, green chilies, and aromatic spices. It's a quick, flavorful, and protein-packed meal perfect for breakfast or a light dinner. üç≥üå∂üçÖ

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tbsp oil or butter
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 medium tomato, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp fresh coriander leaves, chopped

Preparation Steps

  1. Heat Oil: Heat oil or butter in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Saut√© Onions & Chilies: Add chopped onions and green chilies. Saut√© until onions turn translucent.
  3. Add Ginger-Garlic & Tomatoes: Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute. Then add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  4. Add Spices: Add turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for another minute.
  5. Add Eggs: Beat the eggs lightly in a bowl and pour into the pan. Stir continuously to scramble the eggs.
  6. Cook & Season: Cook until eggs are fully set but still moist. Sprinkle garam masala and chopped coriander leaves. Mix gently.
  7. Serve: Serve hot with bread, roti, or as a side dish.

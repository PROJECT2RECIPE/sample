Palak Paneer

Palak Paneer is a classic North Indian vegetarian dish featuring soft paneer cubes cooked in a smooth and flavorful spinach gravy, enriched with aromatic spices and cream. It's a healthy and delicious main course served best with roti or rice. üåøüßÄüçõ

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer (cubed)
  • 300g fresh spinach leaves (palak), washed and chopped
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/4 cup fresh cream or 2 tbsp yogurt (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Preparation Steps

  1. Blanch Spinach: Boil water in a pot, add spinach leaves and cook for 2-3 minutes. Drain and immediately transfer to cold water to retain green color. Blend to a smooth puree and set aside.
  2. Saut√© Spices: Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Cook Onion & Ginger-Garlic: Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown. Then add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies; cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Add Tomatoes & Spices: Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes soften and oil starts to separate.
  5. Add Spinach Puree: Stir in the spinach puree, add water if the gravy is too thick, and simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  6. Add Paneer & Cream: Add paneer cubes and garam masala. Mix gently. Add fresh cream or yogurt if using, and cook for another 2 minutes.
  7. Serve: Garnish with a swirl of cream or butter, and serve hot with roti, naan, or steamed rice.

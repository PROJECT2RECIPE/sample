Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken, also known as Murgh Makhani, is a rich and creamy North Indian dish where tender chicken pieces are simmered in a velvety tomato-based gravy with butter and cream. It's mildly spiced, perfectly balanced in flavor, and best enjoyed with naan or basmati rice. üçóüçõ

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken (cut into cubes)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 large tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Preparation Steps

  1. Marinate Chicken: In a bowl, mix yogurt, lemon juice, red chili powder, turmeric, and salt. Add chicken pieces and marinate for at least 1 hour.
  2. Cook Chicken: Grill or saut√© the marinated chicken until cooked and slightly charred. Set aside.
  3. Prepare Gravy: Heat butter and oil in a pan. Add onions and saut√© until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add Tomatoes: Pour in the tomato puree and cook until the oil separates. Add cumin powder, garam masala, sugar, and salt to taste.
  5. Simmer Chicken: Add the cooked chicken to the gravy. Stir well and let it simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Add Cream: Stir in fresh cream and cook for another 2-3 minutes. Adjust seasoning if needed.
  7. Garnish & Serve: Garnish with coriander leaves and a swirl of cream. Serve hot with naan or rice.

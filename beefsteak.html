Ingredients

  • 2 beef steaks (about 200-250g each)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper, freshly ground
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme or rosemary (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Preparation Steps

  1. Prepare Steaks: Take steaks out of the fridge 30 minutes before cooking to reach room temperature. Pat dry with paper towels.
  2. Season: Rub steaks with olive oil, salt, pepper, garlic, and thyme or rosemary if using. Make sure both sides are well coated.
  3. Heat Pan: Heat a heavy skillet or cast-iron pan over high heat until very hot.
  4. Cook Steaks: Place steaks in the pan and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side for medium-rare, or adjust time to your preferred doneness.
  5. Add Butter & Baste: Add butter to the pan and spoon the melted butter over the steaks while cooking for extra flavor and moisture.
  6. Rest: Remove steaks from heat and let them rest for 5 minutes to allow juices to redistribute.
  7. Serve: Garnish with fresh parsley and serve with your favorite sides like mashed potatoes, salad, or steamed veggies. Enjoy your perfectly cooked beef steak!

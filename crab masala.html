Crab Masala

Ingredients

  • 1 kg fresh crab, cleaned and cut into pieces
  • 2 large onions, finely chopped
  • 2 large tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 3-4 green chilies, slit
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 2 teaspoons red chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 10-12 curry leaves
  • 1/2 cup fresh coconut milk (optional)
  • 3 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Preparation Steps

  1. Heat Oil & Temper: Heat oil in a heavy-bottomed pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter. Add curry leaves and green chilies, sautÃ© for a minute.
  2. SautÃ© Onions: Add chopped onions and cook until golden brown.
  3. Add Ginger-Garlic Paste: Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook until raw smell disappears.
  4. Cook Tomatoes & Spices: Add tomato puree, turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook until oil separates from the masala.
  5. Add Crab: Add the crab pieces and mix well to coat with the masala. Cover and cook on medium heat for 10-12 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Add Coconut Milk & Simmer: Pour in coconut milk (if using) and garam masala. Cook uncovered for another 5 minutes until the crab is fully cooked and the gravy thickens.
  7. Garnish & Serve: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves. Serve hot with steamed rice or your choice of bread. Enjoy this spicy and aromatic crab masala!

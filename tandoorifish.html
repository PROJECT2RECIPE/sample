Tandoori Fish

Tandoori Fish is a classic Indian dish where fish fillets are marinated in a blend of yogurt and spices, then grilled or baked to perfection. It's juicy, smoky, and bursting with bold tandoori flavor‚Äîideal as an appetizer or main course. üêüüî•

Ingredients

  • 500g fish fillets (like tilapia, rohu, or salmon)
  • 1/2 cup thick yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp mustard oil (optional for authentic flavor)
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped coriander and onion rings for garnish

Preparation Steps

  1. Prepare Marinade: In a bowl, combine yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, cumin powder, lemon juice, mustard oil, and salt.
  2. Marinate Fish: Coat the fish fillets evenly in the marinade. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour (or overnight for deeper flavor).
  3. Preheat Grill/Oven: Preheat grill or oven to 200¬∞C (400¬∞F). Line a tray with foil and lightly grease it.
  4. Cook: Place the marinated fish on the tray. Grill or bake for 15‚Äì20 minutes, flipping halfway through, until cooked and slightly charred at edges.
  5. Serve: Garnish with chopped coriander and onion rings. Serve hot with mint chutney and lemon wedges.

Chicken Lollipop

Ingredients

  • 12 chicken wingettes (lollipops), frenched
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chili sauce
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (adjust to taste)
  • 2 tablespoons cornflour
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour (maida)
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Optional: chopped spring onions or coriander for garnish

Preparation Steps

  1. Prepare Chicken: Clean and pat dry the chicken wingettes. Frenched means pushing the meat to one end of the bone to form a lollipop shape.
  2. Marinate: In a bowl, combine soy sauce, chili sauce, ginger-garlic paste, black pepper, red chili powder, and salt. Add the chicken and coat well. Marinate for at least 1 hour in the fridge.
  3. Coat with Flour: Mix cornflour and all-purpose flour in a plate. Take each marinated piece and coat evenly with the flour mix.
  4. Heat Oil & Fry: Heat oil in a deep pan. Deep fry the chicken lollipops on medium heat until golden brown and crispy, about 8-10 minutes. Drain on paper towels.
  5. Garnish & Serve: Garnish with chopped spring onions or coriander. Serve hot with chili sauce or your favorite dip. Enjoy this spicy and crispy appetizer!

Lemon Rice

Lemon Rice is a tangy and flavorful South Indian rice dish made by mixing cooked rice with a tempered blend of mustard seeds, green chilies, curry leaves, and fresh lemon juice. It's a quick, refreshing, and comforting meal or snack. üçãüçöüåø

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cooled)
  • 2 tbsp oil (preferably sesame or peanut oil)
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1-2 dried red chilies
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 10-12 curry leaves
  • 1/4 cup roasted peanuts
  • Juice of 1 large lemon
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves (optional)

Preparation Steps

  1. Heat Oil & Temper: Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter. Then add urad dal, dried red chilies, green chilies, curry leaves, and saut√© until the dals turn golden.
  2. Add Peanuts & Spices: Add roasted peanuts and turmeric powder. Stir for a minute to combine flavors.
  3. Mix Rice: Add the cooked rice and salt. Mix gently but thoroughly so the rice is well coated with the tempered spices.
  4. Add Lemon Juice: Turn off the heat. Add fresh lemon juice and mix well.
  5. Garnish & Serve: Garnish with chopped coriander leaves if desired. Serve warm or at room temperature.
